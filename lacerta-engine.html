Lacerta Engine

My first custom game engine, where I sincerely thought I might be able to make games with it one day. That was before I went down the graphics rabbit hole.

Features :

  • DX11 Forward Renderer
  • PBR/IBL
  • Point Lights
  • Shadow Maps
  • ECS with entt
    • Tesselation & Sum Of Sines Waves
    • Screen Space Reflections
    • Refraction Distortion
